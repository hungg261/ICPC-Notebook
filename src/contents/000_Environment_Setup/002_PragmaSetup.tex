% Optimization \& Diagnostic Pragmas

Enables advanced compiler optimizations, loop unrolling, and vectorization targeted for modern architectures, while silencing diagnostic warnings on older judge systems.
\begin{lstlisting}
#pragma GCC optimize("O3,unroll-loops")
#pragma GCC target("avx2,bmi,bmi2,lzcnt,popcnt")
\end{lstlisting}

Place these optimization lines at the absolute top of your source file, before any include statements. The \lstinline|avx2| enables 256-bit SIMD vectorization for speedups in tight loops.

\begin{lstlisting}
#pragma GCC diagnostic ignored "-Wpragmas"
#pragma GCC diagnostic ignored "-Wunused-result"
#pragma GCC diagnostic ignored "-Wunknown-pragmas"
\end{lstlisting}

The \lstinline|diagnostic| directives prevent compilation warnings or failures on older platforms that do not support modern targets, and silence warnings for ignored return values (e.g., from \lstinline|scanf| or \lstinline|freopen|).
